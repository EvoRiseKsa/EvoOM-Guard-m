## Rules

1. Do not edit, retag, replace, or reinterpret an immutable historical release.
2. A new license must coincide with a new major-version release; this branch
   alone is not a published v4 release.
3. The v4 license applies only to versions and material first distributed with
   it. It does not revoke the rights a recipient obtained under an earlier
   release's license.
4. New private verifier packs, enterprise integrations, control-plane code,
   held-out corpora, customer data, signing keys, and customer materials must
   not be added to public historical repositories.
5. See [THIRD_PARTY.md](THIRD_PARTY.md) before assuming that this repository
   license applies to a particular path.
6. Do not name EvoRise Company as copyright holder or assignee unless a later
   documented assignment supports that statement. The v4 notice instead names
   Mana Alharbi as copyright owner and EvoRise Company as the authorized
   commercial licensing contact; see
   [LICENSE_TRANSITION_V4.md](docs/LICENSE_TRANSITION_V4.md).
